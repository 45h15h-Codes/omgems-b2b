# Features Research

**Domain:** Luxury B2B Diamond & Jewellery Trading
**Researched:** 2026-04-09
**Confidence:** HIGH

## Table Stakes (Must Have)

### 1. Advanced Diamond Filtration
- **The 4Cs**: Carat, Color, Clarity, Cut.
- **Advanced Specs**: Fluorescence, Polish, Symmetry, Table %, Depth %, Lab (GIA/IGI).
- **UI Interaction**: Range sliders for Carat/Price, icon grids for Shape, multi-select for Quality.

### 2. Digital Certification Integration
- Automated GIA/IGI report verification.
- Direct link to laboratory PDF certificates on product pages.
- Data synchronization with lab reports via UUID/Report Number.

### 3. Professional B2B Inventory
- Real-time stock status (Available, On Hold, Reserved, Sold).
- "Hold for Client" feature (typically 24-48 hours).
- Bulk listing management for merchants.

### 4. B2B Transaction Flow
- RFQ (Request for Quotation) / Inquiry system.
- Secure message center for price negotiation.
- Role-based Dashboard (Merchant vs Admin vs Super Admin).

## Differentiators (Luxury Experience)

### 1. Cinematic Visuals (Visual Excellence)
- **360° Stone Interaction**: High-definition interactive viewers (James Allen style).
- **High-End Storytelling**: Contextual "collection" pages with editorial layouts (Cartier style).
- **Micro-Animations**: Ultra-smooth transitions between search and detail views.

### 2. Merchant Verification & Trust
- Verified merchant badges.
- Transparent transaction history for B2B partners.
- Escrow-style payment protection for high-value orders.

### 3. Guided Curation
- "Personalized Selection" for premium buyers.
- Virtual appointment scheduling integrated into the platform.

## Anti-Features (Deliberately Not Building)

- **Social Media Shopping**: Avoid "Like" or "Share" clutter that degrades the luxury feel.
- **Aggressive Popups**: No discount spinners or aggressive FOMO triggers (e.g., "10 people looking at this").
- **Generic Feedback**: Replacing star ratings with professional testimonials or verification logs.
